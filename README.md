Traditional emergency systems only alert people about danger but fail to provide personalized guidance for visually impaired individuals.

During disasters, visually impaired people face major challenges such as:
- Difficulty identifying hazards
- Trouble locating exits
- Lack of navigation assistance
- Delayed rescue support

NAYAN AI addresses these challenges using intelligent real-time assistance powered by AI.

## ⚙️ Workflow
1. User activates NAYAN AI through voice command
2. Live camera scans surroundings
3. AI detects hazards and identifies safe paths
4. Voice guidance assists evacuation
5. Emergency alerts are sent to contacts/rescue teams
6. Rescue teams receive live victim intelligence
